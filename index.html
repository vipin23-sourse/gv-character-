Waleef’s inspiration

Our main character is based on the beautiful Arabian Wolf. Now extinct in the UAE, these creatures were once prolific in the region.

Wolves mate for life and have strong family units. Many leadership and teamwork lessons are derived from wolf pack behavior. Not only astute, they are known to be playful and curious, making them a perfect animal model for the GV ambassador.

Zoya’s inspiration

Our main hero’s best friend is based on the beautiful Sand Cat.

Native to the desert. An endangered species, the sand cat is thought to be nocturnal and is known for their speed and ability to travel long distances.

Oska’s inspiration

Our comedic sidekick is a Quokka.

Our comedic sidekick is a Quokka, widely thought to be the “happiest animal in the world” they come from Western Australia

Ayah’s inspiration

As the National Bird of the UAE the Falcon is a symbol of force and courage.

Aya is loosely based on a Peregrine Falcon who travel widely outside the nesting season their name means "wanderer" and they can fly as many as 15,500 miles in a year. Yet they have an incredible homing instinct that leads them back to favored aeries.
